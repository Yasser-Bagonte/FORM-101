Exercise is any planned physical activity that helps improve health and fitness. It benefits the body by strengthening the heart, muscles, and bones, helps manage weight, reduces stress, boosts mood, and prevents diseases. Common types include aerobic (like running), strength training, flexibility (like stretching), and balance exercises. Regular exercise is important for both kids and adults to stay healthy and active.
